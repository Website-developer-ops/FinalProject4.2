Bodybuilding Exercise Guide

Comprehensive Workout Plan for Strength, Size, and Recovery

1. Structuring Your Workout Plan

  • Split Training: Divide workouts into muscle groups (e.g., Push/Pull/Legs, or Chest-Back-Arms-Legs).
  • Weekly Frequency: Train each muscle group at least twice per week for optimal growth.
  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weights or reps weekly.
  • Rest Days: Take at least 1-2 full rest days per week to allow muscle repair.

2. Training for Recovery

  • Sleep 7–9 hours nightly.
  • Consume sufficient protein (1.6–2.2g per kg of body weight).
  • Hydrate well (2–3 liters daily).
  • Incorporate active recovery: light cardio, stretching, or yoga.
  • Avoid training the same muscle group on consecutive days.

3. Barbell Exercises

Barbell Squat

Muscles Targeted: Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings, Core

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 6–10 reps

Suggested Weight: Intermediate: 60–80% of 1RM

Barbell Deadlift

Muscles Targeted: Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back, Traps

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 5–8 reps

Suggested Weight: Intermediate: 70–85% of 1RM

Barbell Bench Press

Muscles Targeted: Chest, Triceps, Front Shoulders

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 6–10 reps

Suggested Weight: Intermediate: 60–75% of 1RM

4. Dumbbell Exercises

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Muscles Targeted: Shoulders, Triceps

Sets & Reps: 3 sets x 8–12 reps

Suggested Weight: Choose a weight that allows full range of motion

Dumbbell Row

Muscles Targeted: Lats, Rhomboids, Rear Deltoids

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 10–12 reps

Suggested Weight: Moderate to heavy

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Muscles Targeted: Side Deltoids

Sets & Reps: 3 sets x 12–15 reps

Suggested Weight: Light to moderate

5. Machine-Based Exercises

Leg Press Machine

Muscles Targeted: Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 10–12 reps

Suggested Weight: Moderate to heavy

Chest Press Machine

Muscles Targeted: Chest, Triceps

Sets & Reps: 3–4 sets x 8–10 reps

Suggested Weight: Moderate

Seated Row Machine

Muscles Targeted: Back (Lats, Traps), Biceps

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 10–12 reps

Suggested Weight: Moderate

6. Calf Muscle Training

Standing Calf Raise (Machine or Barbell)

Muscles Targeted: Gastrocnemius

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 15–20 reps

Suggested Weight: Moderate to heavy

Seated Calf Raise (Machine or Dumbbell on Knee)

Muscles Targeted: Soleus

Sets & Reps: 4 sets x 20–25 reps

Suggested Weight: Moderate

Single-Leg Dumbbell Calf Raise

Muscles Targeted: Entire Calf

Sets & Reps: 3 sets x 15–20 reps per leg

Suggested Weight: Light to moderate (for balance)

7. Tips for All Levels

  • Beginners: Start with full-body workouts 3x/week. Focus on form before weight.
  • Intermediate: Use split training. Track progress weekly.
  • Advanced: Add intensity techniques like supersets, dropsets, and periodization.
